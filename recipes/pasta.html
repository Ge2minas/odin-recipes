Pasta Recipe

Description

This recipe takes inspiration from a classic pasta al limone by using heavy cream as the base for the luscious and decadent sauce.

It comes together in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 c. torn pitted Castelvetrano or green olives
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/3 c. gin or vodka
  • 2/3 c. heavy cream
  • 2/3 c. finely grated Parmesan
  • 1/3 c. chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tbsp. unsalted butter

Steps

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook spaghetti, stirring occasionally, until al dente according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add olives, garlic, and lemon zest and cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Add gin and cook, stirring occasionally, until reduced by about half, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add cream, olive brine, pasta, and 1/2 cup reserved pasta water. Cook, tossing constantly and adding more pasta water as needed, until pasta is coated and sauce is emulsified, about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in Parmesan, parsley, and butter until butter is melted; season with salt. Top with more Parmesan before serving.
